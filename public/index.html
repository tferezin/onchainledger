What We Detect

Comprehensive risk analysis across 10 critical dimensions

Authority Risks

Flags tokens where supply can be inflated or wallets can be frozen at any time.

Holder Concentration

Identifies when top wallets hold dangerous amounts that could crash the price.

Low Liquidity

Warns when there's not enough liquidity to exit your position safely.

Honeypot Traps

Detects tokens you can buy but cannot sell. Saves you from total loss.

Dangerous Extensions

Identifies hidden token features that can be used against you.

Unlocked Liquidity

Checks if liquidity can be pulled at any moment (rug pull risk).

Insider Activity

Detects when creators or insiders sniped their own launch.

Coordinated Wallets

Identifies multiple wallets controlled by the same entity.

Pump & Dump Patterns

Analyzes price history for manipulation signals and volume anomalies.

Token Age

New tokens carry higher risk. Age is factored into the score.
